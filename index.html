About Me

A Teacher Who
Truly Cares

My name is Jerin Hany Tahsina Jotee, and English is more than a subject I teach — it is a language I love. For over four and a half years, I have dedicated myself to helping school and college students discover their voice in English, overcome their fears of grammar, and build the confidence they need to succeed academically and beyond.

I believe that every student learns differently, which is why I tailor my teaching approach to meet each individual where they are. Whether a student is struggling with basic grammar rules or preparing for board examinations, I provide patient, structured, and encouraging guidance that makes learning both effective and enjoyable.

With a strong academic background — from SSC through HSC to a Masters degree — and hands-on experience at respected institutions including Technical School and College and Technical Teachers' Training College, I bring both theoretical expertise and practical insight to every classroom.
